Surströmming is a traditional Swedish dish that consists of fermented Baltic herring. It is known for its strong and pungent odor, earning it the reputation of being one of the smelliest foods in the world. The fermentation process involves preserving the herring in brine for several months, allowing beneficial bacteria to break down the fish proteins and create a distinct flavor.

Lasagna
Stuffing (beef, pork or mix of meat) - 500 g
Onions - 1 pc.
Garlic - 2 cloves
Tomato sauce - 800 g
Tomato paste - 2 tbsp.
Milk - 500 ml
Cream - 200 ml
Parmesan (or other hard cheese) - 200 gr
Mozzarella - 200 gr
Spices (salt, pepper, basil, oregano) - to taste
Olive oil - for frying


Recipe: Preheat the oven to 180°C. In a large frying pan heat the olive oil and fry the chopped onion and garlic until translucent. Add the minced meat and fry, breaking up the lumps in the meat until it turns golden. Pour in the tomato sauce and add the tomato paste. Season with salt, pepper, basil and oregano to taste. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and cook under cover for about 20 minutes. In a separate bowl, mix together the milk and cream. In a deep baking tray, spread a small amount of the stuffing sauce on the bottom. Spread a layer of lasagna sheets evenly on top, then spread another layer of sauce. Repeat these layers until all the lasagna sheets and sauce are used. Finish the last layer of lasagna with the sauce and sprinkle with grated parmesan and sliced mozzarella. Place the tray in the preheated oven and cook for about 30-40 minutes, until the top is golden and bubbles appear on the sides. Once cooked, it is recommended to let the lasagna rest for a few minutes before serving. Cut into portions and enjoy!
